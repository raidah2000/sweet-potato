Ramly Burger Rasa Ayam Panggang

Burger Ramly is one of my favourite foods. The latest version of Burger Ramly is Ramly Burger Rasa Ayam Panggang and Ramly Burger Rasa Daging Panggang. Both tasted good but I prefer ayam panggang. Its easy to make and tasted very good. When I make Ramly Burger I will add extra chili powder and curry powder as their spices. About the sauces I love to add Chili thousand island sauces with extra spicy. Other than that, the best way to take a break at night is make or order some Ramly Burger and eat it while checking our works. It is the best vibes while doing an assignments.
